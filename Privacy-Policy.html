Information We Collect

When you use MyGrammar AI, we collect only the minimum personal information necessary to provide our services. This includes your email address, which is used solely for OTP-based authentication and account access. We do not ask for your name, phone number, address, or any demographic details.

When you enter text into the App for grammar checking, the text is processed to provide corrections. These inputs are not permanently stored by default. Grammar corrections are saved to your history only when you explicitly press the "Save" button. Unsaved text and corrections are not retained.

Usage Information

We may collect limited usage-related information such as feature usage frequency (for example, number of grammar checks used per day or hour) to enforce free-tier limits (5 checks per day and up to 2 checks per hour) and to ensure proper functioning of the App. This data is used only for operational purposes.

We may automatically collect basic device-related information such as device type, operating system version, and app version. This information helps us maintain compatibility, improve performance, and fix bugs. We do not use this information to identify you personally.
